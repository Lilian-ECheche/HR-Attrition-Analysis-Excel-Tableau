 HR Excel Attrition Dashboard

This dashboard was built using Microsoft Excel to analyze employee attrition across different factors such as job role, tenure, age, income, and job satisfaction.

Key Insights:

* Sales Representatives have the highest attrition rate (39.8%)
* Employees working overtime have significantly higher attrition (30.5%)
* Employees aged 18–25 show the highest turnover (35.8%)
* Low income employees (under 5k) have higher attrition (21.8%)
* Poor work life balance leads to the highest attrition (31.3%)
